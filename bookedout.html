    Yes — that's the engine. The full version inside the command center adds saved scenarios, package presets, PDF export, and the negotiation playbook for every output it generates.

    Right after checkout you'll get an email with your login link to the command center. Bookmark it — that's your lifetime access. Every tool, template, script, video, and resource lives there.
