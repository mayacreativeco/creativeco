// questions

Questions.

Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — one click, no "please stay" emails, no retention call. You keep access until the end of your current billing period.
What if I miss a live call? +
Every Deal Clinic is recorded and dropped in the vault within 24 hours. You can also submit questions in advance and I'll answer them live — so you still get your answer even if you can't attend.
Do I need the Kit first? +
Not required, but it helps. The Kit gives you the foundations — rate calculator, core scripts, the proposal template. The Club is ongoing implementation support layered on top.
Is there a community? +
Yes — a private channel for members only. Small, working creators, no lurkers, no coaches. It's not the whole point of the membership, but it's a real one.
What platform is it on? +
Membership and vault are hosted on Stan. Deal Clinic calls happen on Zoom. Community lives in a private channel you'll get access to once you join.
Do prices ever go up? +
Yes — after the first 50 founding members, pricing moves to $49/mo and $497/yr. Founding members lock in today's rate for as long as they're an active member.
