// questions

Questions.

How soon can we book? +
Usually within 1–2 weeks of your application being accepted. I hold a limited number of intensive slots each month, so timing depends on availability when you apply.
What if I'm pre-revenue? +
If you haven't booked any paid UGC work yet, the Booked Out Kit is a better starting point — it gives you the foundational tools. Come back for the intensive once you've shipped a few deals and want to scale up.
Do you offer refunds? +
Because of the prep work involved, intensives are non-refundable once the call is booked. If something urgent comes up, I'll reschedule once at no cost.
What happens after the 14 days? +
Most clients roll into the UGC Systems Club for ongoing implementation support, or book a second intensive a few months later. Some move into the 4-Week Advisory container if they want heavier hands-on support.
Can I bring my business partner? +
Yes — for creator-founder duos or small UGC agencies, bringing one partner is fine. More than that and we should talk about the 4-Week Advisory instead.
Can this turn into a retainer? +
The Strategy Intensive is one-and-done by design. If you want ongoing support after, the next step is the 4-Week Advisory container — that's the structured retainer-style offer.
