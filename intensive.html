// faq

Common questions.

How is this different from the Booked Out Kit?

+
The Kit gives you the tools — calculators, templates, scripts, the whole operator stack. The Intensive gives you the strategy and personal coaching to apply them to your specific business. Most clients run the Kit alongside the Intensive — the tools become more powerful with personalized direction. If you don't have the Kit yet, you'll get the most out of the Intensive if you do.

Why three sessions instead of more or fewer?

+
One session isn't enough to actually move a business. Weekly sessions create dependency and don't leave time for real implementation between calls. Three sessions across a quarter gives us time to set strategy, implement, and measure results — three real decision points instead of one. It's designed to get you running independently, not to keep you in a coaching relationship.

Do you take everyone who applies?

+
No. The Intensive only works if you're positioned to actually implement what we build. If you're not at the right stage — typically $2K+/month from UGC and ready to execute on systems — I'll tell you, and suggest the Kit or a different path. The application is how we make sure you're a fit and that the quarter will actually move the needle for you.

What if I need more support after the quarter?

+
Some clients renew for a second quarter at the same rate. Some move to async-only advisory. Some graduate and run it entirely on their own — which is the goal. By the end of the quarter you should have the dashboard, the workflow, and the skills to keep scaling without needing me in the room.

Can I pay in installments?

+
Yes — split into two payments of $625, at the start of months 1 and 2 of the quarter. Just mention it in your application and I'll set it up before our first session.
